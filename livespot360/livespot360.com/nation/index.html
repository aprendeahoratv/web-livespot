Our
experiences

Uncode

UNCODE is an immersive tech-centered lifestyle event aimed at uniting tech enthusiasts, content creators, thought leaders, and key stakeholders in the creative and tech sectors.

Themed “Influencing Culture through Technology,” UNCODE featured renowned speakers like Iddris Sandu, a 22-year-old cultural architect known for collaborations with Google, Uber, and Snapchat. The initiative garnered massive registrations with an impressive 80% turnout rate on the day of the event. Positive feedback poured in from sponsors, speakers, and attendees alike, affirming the event’s impact and the quality of its content.
 

Love Like A
Movie 1, 2 & 3

Our objective was to conceive an unrivaled, unforgettable Valentine’s spectacle that catalyzed distinctive interactions and transcended the prevailing benchmarks in West Africa’s experiential marketing landscape.

We masterminded a remarkable fusion of dramatic theatre, melodic opera, vibrant choreography, potent thematic storytelling, and vigorous performances. Leveraging a holistic sales and marketing approach and graced by the presence of illustrious guests such as Kim Kardashian, Kelly Rowland, and Ciara, we ceaselessly pushed the envelope with each successive event.

Love Like a Movie not only broke all pre-existing records but also blazed new trails in terms of technical production and audience engagement across its three remarkable editions.

Entertainment Week Lagos

Entertainment Week Lagos is a convergence designed to nurture talent, enhance skills, build capacity, foster local and international collaborations, drive cultural and social change, and facilitate access for both local and global exchange in the entertainment landscape.

Entertainment Week Lagos 2023 concluded with nearly 100 speakers, 20 pop-ups, 10 workshops, 30 short films, 2 keynotes, 3 think tanks, 15 masterclasses, 10 panel sessions, 35 exhibition spaces, and 120 showcases. The annual 8-day extravaganza garnered over 105 million in PR value, 20 million in audience reach, and over 10,000 attendees. It successfully established Lagos as a burgeoning epicenter for world-class entertainment.

Born In Africa Festival

With a vision to retell the African story through an exposé of a myriad of African talents, we launched the largest festival in Nigeria in December 2018 and 2019 named Born in Africa Festival. Forming a strategic alliance with Access Bank, we themed the event “GIVING THE WORLD ACCESS TO AFRICA”.

With over 20,000 people in attendance and millions more post event, we were able to turn international attention to Africa, through the eyes of Nigeria.
